Crop Management Tips

Water Management

Proper irrigation is crucial for crop success. Different crops have different water requirements. Consider drip irrigation for water conservation.

Pest Control

Implement integrated pest management (IPM) strategies to minimize chemical use while effectively controlling pests.

Soil Health

Maintain soil health through crop rotation, cover crops, and appropriate fertilization based on soil tests.

Crop Rotation

Rotate crops to prevent soil depletion, reduce pest pressure, and improve overall farm productivity.
